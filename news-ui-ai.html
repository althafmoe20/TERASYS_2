UI opens undergraduate study program in artificial intelligence

October 4, 2025 TERASYS Newsroom

The University of Indonesia (UI) has officially announced the opening of a new undergraduate study program in Artificial Intelligence. This initiative aims to prepare students for the growing demand for AI professionals in Indonesia and globally.

The program will cover fundamental and advanced topics in AI, including machine learning, data science, robotics, and ethical considerations in technology. UI hopes this program will foster innovation and contribute to the nation's digital transformation.

"We are committed to equipping our students with the skills needed to lead in the era of artificial intelligence," said a UI representative.

Registration for the new program will open soon, and prospective students are encouraged to stay updated through the university's official channels.
