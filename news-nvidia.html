Nvidia Boss: Chinese Chips Are Just Behind US

October 4, 2025 TERASYS Newsroom

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang stated that Chinese chipmakers are making rapid progress and are just behind the US in the global semiconductor race. Speaking at a recent event, Huang emphasized the importance of healthy competition and innovation in the industry.

"Based on these achievements, the US should allow its companies to compete in China," Huang said. He highlighted that the advancements made by Chinese companies are impressive and that the global market benefits from open competition.

The comments come amid ongoing tensions between the US and China over technology and trade, particularly in the semiconductor sector. Huang believes that collaboration and fair competition will drive further innovation and growth for all players in the industry.

"Competition is good for everyone. It pushes us to be better and to innovate faster," Huang added.

Nvidia continues to lead in GPU and AI chip development, while keeping a close eye on emerging competitors from China and other regions.
